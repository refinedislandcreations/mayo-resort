We Craft Meal Plans

How it works

Step 1: Choose Your Plan

Every week, we offer a variety of meal plans to suit different goals and preferences, including:

  • Balanced
  • High Protein
  • Vegan
  • Carnivore
  • Custom

Each menu features five meal options per day, providing you with a total of 15 meals to choose from.

Step 2: Mix & Match

You can select meals from one menu or mix and match across all the options.

For example, choose three meals from the Balanced menu, or combine meals from High Protein, Balanced, and Vegan.

The choice is yours!

Step 3: Align With Your Goals

Each meal comes with detailed macros, so you can select meals based on your daily calorie and macronutrient goals.

Whether you’re aiming to balance your intake, increase protein, or follow a plant-based diet, we’ve got you covered.

Step 4: Custom Meal Plans

Need something more specific? Our Custom Plans are tailored just for you.

Share your desired macros, or let us help you identify them, and we’ll create a meal plan that fits your needs.

Custom plans can also accommodate medical requirements or food preferences, ensuring you get meals that are both nutritious and suitable for your lifestyle.

Delivery

We deliver daily from Monday to Saturday in an hour window that we inform you of in advance, and Sunday is our off day.

If you like to have meals seven days per week, we can deliver Sunday meals on Saturday.
